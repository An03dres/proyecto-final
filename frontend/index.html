Traditional Style. Classic and Bold.

The traditional tattoo style or "old school," features thick lines, vibrant colors, and iconic motifs like anchors and roses. It represents a nostalgic and timeless aesthetic.

Blackout. Dark and Powerful.

The Blackout style involves large areas of solid black ink. It's striking and dramatic, perfect for those seeking a bold and minimalist design.

Realism. Living Art.

The realism tattoo style aims to mimic photographs with precise details and subtle shading. It captures vivid, lifelike images on the skin.
